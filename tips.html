Unity Tips & Tricks

Helpful guidance for beginners and resources to level up your Unity skills.

1
Save Often

Always save your project regularly to prevent losing work. Use
Ctrl + S (Windows) or Cmd + S (Mac).

2
Organize Assets

Keep your assets organized in folders such as Scripts, Materials, and Prefabs to maintain a clean project structure.

3
Use Prefabs

Create prefabs for objects that are reused multiple times to maintain consistency and efficiency.

4
Keep Scripts Simple

Write short, readable scripts. Avoid monolithic scripts to make debugging and reuse easier.

5
Test Lighting

Always check your lighting in Play Mode to ensure your scenes look balanced and realistic.
