Unity Simple Example: Create a Sphere

Step-by-step guide to creating a sphere and applying a material in Unity.

1Add a Sphere

In the Unity menu, go to GameObject → 3D Object → Sphere to add a sphere to your scene. This will create a new sphere at the center of your scene view.

2Create a Material

Right-click in the Project window → Create → Material. Name the material BlueMaterial and in the Inspector, change its color to blue. This material will be applied to the sphere to change its appearance.

3Apply Material

Drag and drop BlueMaterial onto your sphere in the Scene. The sphere will now appear with the blue material applied, showing how materials affect object appearance in Unity.
